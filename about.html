Our Mission

Democratizing Education for Everyone, Everywhere

At Stackly, we believe that quality education should not be a privilege reserved for the few. Our mission is to break down barriers to learning by providing world-class, industry-relevant courses at accessible price points. We envision a world where anyone, regardless of their background or location, can acquire the skills needed to thrive in the digital economy.

We are committed to fostering a global community of lifelong learners who are equipped with practical, hands-on knowledge. Through innovative teaching methodologies, cutting-edge technology platforms, and unwavering student support, we aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

Our Vision

Building the Future of Digital Education

By 2030, Stackly aims to become the world's most trusted platform for skill-based learning. We envision a future where traditional degrees are complemented by micro-credentials and stackable certifications that accurately reflect an individual's capabilities. Our platform will serve as the bridge between ambition and achievement, connecting learners with opportunities across the globe.

Our Journey

Growth Timeline

From a small startup idea to a global educational powerhouse — trace our remarkable journey.

2018

The Beginning

Stackly was founded in a small co-working space in Salem, Tamil Nadu. Started with just 3 courses and a vision to transform online education in India.

3 Courses 12 Students
2019

First Major Milestone

Crossed 1,000 active students. Launched our proprietary learning management system with interactive coding environments and real-time collaboration tools.

15 Courses 1,200 Students
2020

Pandemic Pivot

During the global pandemic, we expanded rapidly to meet surging demand for online education. Launched free community programs and partnered with government skill initiatives.

45 Courses 8,500 Students
2021

International Expansion

Opened our first international office in Singapore. Launched courses in 5 languages and established partnerships with universities in Southeast Asia.

80 Courses 22,000 Students
2022

AI Integration

Introduced AI-powered personalized learning paths, automated code review systems, and intelligent career recommendation engines to enhance student outcomes.

120 Courses 35,000 Students
2023

Enterprise Solutions

Launched Stackly for Business — corporate training solutions for Fortune 500 companies. Achieved ISO 27001 certification for data security excellence.

150 Courses 42,000 Students
2024

Global Recognition

Named "EdTech Innovator of the Year" by the Global Education Summit. Crossed 50,000 active learners across 45 countries. Launched scholarship programs for underprivileged students.

200+ Courses 50,000+ Students
2025

The Future Ahead

Expanding into AR/VR immersive learning experiences, blockchain-verified credentials, and metaverse classrooms. Targeting 100,000 learners by year-end.
